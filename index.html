Why Use CSV for Bank Statements?

CSV (Comma-Separated Values) is one of the most widely used formats in accounting and finance. It allows users to import data into Excel, Google Sheets, QuickBooks, and other financial platforms. Unlike PDFs—which are typically non-editable and difficult to extract data from—CSV files are flexible and allow you to sort, filter, and analyze your financial data with ease.
